Project Description:

Seatrash

Trash, collected from the ocean, lakes, and rivers, is cast in resin to form hanging sculptures.
As an installation, the trash hangs from motors, which change the height in response to realtime data collected from weather buoys around the world's oceans. The result is a realtime, data-driven, floating "sea of trash" that ebbs and flows with the tide.
