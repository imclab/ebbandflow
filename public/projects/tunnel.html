Project Description:

"Tunnel in the Nocturnal Hum"

Beginning with a single picture, the image is photographed, and re-photographed, thousands of times until the amplified imperfections create an entirely new landscape. Played back as a stop-motion animation, this video is a documentation of an image breaking apart with each frame.
